ML Model Info

🧠 Logistic Regression Classifier

This system uses a pure NumPy Logistic Regression trained on 8 hand-engineered resume features. The model learns from your manual shortlist/reject decisions and automatically rescores all pending applicants.

FeatureDescriptionWeight
Keyword Match% of job keywords found in resume30%
Tech SkillsCount of 50+ tech skills detected20%
ExperienceYears of experience (extracted via regex)15%
Education LevelPhD=4, Masters=3, Bachelors=2, etc.10%
Soft SkillsLeadership, communication, etc.10%
AchievementsQuantified results (%, $, numbers)5%
Section CompletenessHas education/skills/projects sections5%
Resume LengthWord count (normalized to 800 words)5%
🔁 Active Learning: Review at least 10 applications manually (shortlist / reject) to trigger Logistic Regression training. Until then, the weighted heuristic is used.
